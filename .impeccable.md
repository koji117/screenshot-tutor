## Design Context

### Users
A solo developer studying for certifications, on macOS at a desk during long reading sessions. Often pastes textbook pages, slide decks, code snippets, and exam problems. Bilingual: reads English and Japanese.

### Brand Personality
**Calm. Scholarly. Patient.**

This is a quiet study companion, not an AI tool that demands attention. It treats the user's time as serious — long reading sessions, focused work. Warm but disciplined. The screenshot is the primary citizen; the app's chrome should recede.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Refined editorial — a quiet study desk in a library.**

- Light mode default (long reading sessions favor lighter backgrounds; reduces eye strain in lit rooms).
- Generous whitespace, careful spacing rhythm.
- Distinctive typography: a warm editorial serif paired with a clean geometric sans. Both must work in EN and JA.
- Restrained color: warm off-white background, dark ink foreground, one deep ink-blue accent. Tinted neutrals (no pure white/black).
- No gradients, no glassmorphism, no glowing cards.

**References:** Are.na, Read.cv, classic literary journals. The chat bubbles should feel like marginal notes in a textbook, not Slack messages.

**Anti-references:** Generic AI app aesthetics (cyan-on-dark, neon accents, gradient text). Default Inter/system-stack typography. ChatGPT-style chat UI. Templated dashboard card grids.

### Design Principles
1. **Reading is the primary act.** The summary is the focus; everything else recedes.
2. **Quiet over loud.** Restrained palette, restrained typography, no decorations that demand attention.
3. **Tool, not toy.** Functional and dignified. No gamified affordances or playful touches.
4. **Bilingual-aware.** Typography pair must work for both Latin and CJK with consistent rhythm.
5. **Respect the screenshot.** The user's pasted image is a primary citizen, not a thumbnail.
